Join us

IBSG welcomes people from different cultural and academic backgrounds. An in-depth understanding of Finance is not a must. We especially value the insights into Natural Science/Engineering/Social Science.

IBSG is mainly referral-based. As such, it is highly recommended that you reach out to your current network and find your referral who is a current member of IBSG. The member will walk you through the very details and assist you with the application process.

Though referral-based, we always open the door to talents with passion. You could also apply with your CV and 1-page cover letter to ibsghk@gmail.com. We will process your request within 2 weeks.
